Make your desktop beautiful

  • Give your homescreen some sweet fairy dust
  • Lightweight app with simple UI
  • Different resolutions up to 8k
  • Crop and rotate image before setting
  • Change home wallpaper and lock screen background
  • Pick your favourites, browse categories, search and use filters to find your best wallpaper

Wallpool is a free application with a huge number of beautiful wallpapers, we have all the resolutions HD, Full HD, 2k, 4k and up to 8k.

That's right, you see every time you pick up the phone. Our goal is to make the phone more enjoyable, because it is pleasant to use the phone when a beautiful HD picture with a resolution of up to 8 thousand is displayed on the screen, which you have chosen.

In addition, this process takes place quickly, at ease, and using the filters you need. To do this, we have a huge database of pictures, which replenishes every day with flexible filters.

We also connected a large number of high-quality images to the https://wallhaven.cc service, in the future we plan to add other services.

So welcome to our app with fantastic wallpapers. Enjoy and choose your wallpaper.
